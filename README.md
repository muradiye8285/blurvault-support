BlurVault Privacy Policy
BlurVault is designed to protect your privacy.

Data Collection
BlurVault does not collect, store, or share personal data.

Photos and Processing
All photo processing happens on-device.
Your photos never leave your device.

Tracking and Analytics
BlurVault uses no tracking and no analytics SDKs.

Advertising
BlurVault shows no ads.

Permissions
BlurVault may request Photos add-only permission only when you choose to save an edited image.
